Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter has been orphaned and lives in the house of his abominable uncles and unbearable cousin Dudley. Harry feels very sad and alone, until one day he receives a letter that will change his life forever. In it they inform him that he has been accepted as a student at Hogwarts School of Magic. From that moment on, Harry's luck takes a spectacular turn. In that very special school he will learn incantations, fabulous tricks and defense tactics against evil arts. He will become the school champion of Quidditch, a type of aerial football played on broomsticks, and will make a handful of good friends... but also some fearsome enemies. But, above all, he will know the secrets that will allow him to fulfill his destiny. Well, although he may not seem like it at first glance, Harry is not an ordinary boy: he is a real wizard!.
