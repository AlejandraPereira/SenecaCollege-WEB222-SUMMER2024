When Dr. Frederick Starks, a psychoanalyst with a long career behind him, receives this mysterious and disturbing anonymous message, his tranquility and predictable life falls apart. Starks will have to use all his cunning and speed to, in fifteen days, find out who is the author of the massive threat that promises to make his existence impossible. If he does not succeed, he must choose between committing suicide or witnessing how, one after another, his family, friends and acquaintances are being murdered by a psychopath who promises to carry out the plan he has devised to take revenge to the end. Giving an unexpected twist to the relationship between doctor and patient, John Katzenbach offers us a fascinating novel that connects with the tradition of the best psychological suspense.
